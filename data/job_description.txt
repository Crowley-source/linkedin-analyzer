Data Analyst — Growth & Product Team

About the Role
We are looking for a Data Analyst to join our fast-growing Product Analytics team. You will work closely with product managers, engineers, and business stakeholders to translate complex data into clear, actionable insights that drive decision-making across the company.

Responsibilities
- Design and maintain dashboards and reports to track key product and business metrics
- Write complex SQL queries to extract, clean, and analyze large datasets
- Conduct A/B testing analysis and communicate results to non-technical stakeholders
- Identify trends, patterns, and anomalies in data to surface business opportunities
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define metrics and measurement frameworks
- Build and maintain data models using dbt or similar tools
- Present findings and recommendations to senior leadership

Requirements
- 2+ years of experience in a data analyst or business analyst role
- Strong proficiency in SQL (window functions, CTEs, subqueries)
- Experience with Python (Pandas, NumPy) for data manipulation and analysis
- Hands-on experience with BI tools such as Tableau, Looker, or Power BI
- Solid understanding of statistics, A/B testing, and experimental design
- Experience with data visualization and storytelling for business audiences
- Strong communication skills — ability to explain complex analyses clearly
- Familiarity with dbt, Airflow, or similar data pipeline tools is a plus

Nice to Have
- Experience with cloud data warehouses (BigQuery, Snowflake, Redshift)
- Knowledge of product analytics frameworks (funnel analysis, cohort analysis, retention)
- Background in SaaS, e-commerce, or consumer tech
